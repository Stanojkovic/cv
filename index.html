   About Me

I was born in 1978. in Leskovac. I graduated from the secondary technical school, the direction "Mechanical Engineering and Metal Processing" as a student of the generation of all grades.

I am a member of Leskovac School of comics since its establishment in 1995. I was actively involved in drawing by 1997 when I enrolled at the Military Academy in Belgrade. I published short comic books in local newspapers and comic magazines such as Naša reč, Đački zabavnik, Arsenal and Parabellum. During the studies and the service, I illustrated several textbooks for the Military Academy.

In 2001, I graduated at the Military Academy as the best in class ABHO. I am retired military officer.

I am currently studying mvc asp .net technology on Faculty of Electronic Engineering in Nis !
